I am Amed Mvoulana,
PhD researcher and Teaching Assistant
at LIGM and Université Gustave Eiffel.

My main interest lies in Computer Vision, Mathematics, Medical Image Analysis and Machine Learning.

My research focuses on the development of vision-based intelligent tools for the early screening and diagnosis of ocular diseases. Working from retinal images, we cover the detection of the early signs related to the most prevalent ocular conditions, including diabetic retinopathy (DR), age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and glaucoma. These tools aim at enhancing the diagnosis from the ophthalmologist, and ensuring global access to eye health services.
